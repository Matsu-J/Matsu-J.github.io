Ulliksella tavataan!


What?

Kumpulan Wappu brings all student of Kumpula together to celebrate Wappu for the whole duration of April. Student organizations of Kumpula will be organizing events throughout April. To incentivize participating in these events there is a possibility to purchase a Wappu pass with which you can get an overall badge by taking part in events! There will be all kinds of events to come and there will also be free events.

You can get stamps for the pass from participating in events held by Kumpula organizations. The pass is completed by participating in eight Wappu events of which at least four are from different organizations. The list of events that are in the pass can be found on this website. The stamp can be received during the event from a member of WappuCrew or the person responsible for the event. When you turn in your pass you will receive a “Wappu 2026” badge and the first completion badge W if you completed the pass successfully.

The completion badges can be received in the following order: W - A - B - U. The requirements for earning the completion badge remain the same every year.

The price of Wappu Pass is 9 € and with a plastic cover 10,5 €. The price of the pass includes the pass, a lanyard, a “Wappu 2026” badge and upon successful completion the completion badge. Lost or destroyed passes will not be replaced.

Where?

The pass can be bought from Kide.app.

The passes will be given out on the Kumpula campus in Physicum.

Information regarding the giving out of badges will be announced later.

When?

The sale of passes will start on March 23rd at noon. The sale ends on April 10 at 23.55.
